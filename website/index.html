OBJECTIVES OF LITTLE FLOWERS PUBLIC SCHOOL

  • To provide education which includes sound learning and building of good moral character of children and youth.
  • To work for the welfare of the youth and children to bring about an all round development at this formative period on their life to enable them to become better citizens of future.

STUPENDOUS SUCCESS OF LITTLE FLORIANS

The outstanding students of the Little Flowers Public School, Kabir Nagar attained 100% result in class X CBSE Exam, 2016-17 and brought laurels to the school. The brilliant students who have shown marvelous performance by scoring 11 CGPA are Abhishek Kumar, Bhavya Sharma, Deepanshi, Vaibhav Kumar, Akanksha Aggarwal, Anubhav Parashar, Gaurav Tiwari, Harshvardhan Tyagi, Nikhil Bansal, Piyush Sharma and Trishal Bhardwaj. 36 Students got 9 and above CGPA.

The School Founder & Director , Smt. C.M Patel, Chairman Mr. R.K Dua , Manager Dr. Sweena Arora and Vice-Principal Mrs. Anita Mahajan congratulated the students and wished for their bright future.
